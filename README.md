# 😊 Customer Sentiment Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer reviews to understand customer opinions and identify whether the overall sentiment is positive, negative, or neutral.

The analysis helps businesses understand customer satisfaction and improve products and services based on customer feedback.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer reviews
- Identify customer sentiment
- Understand customer satisfaction
- Support business decision-making

---

## 📂 Dataset

Customer Reviews Dataset

---

## 📊 Expected Output

- Positive Reviews
- Negative Reviews
- Neutral Reviews
- Customer Satisfaction Insights

---

## 📈 Business Benefits

- Improve customer experience
- Identify customer issues
- Support business growth
- Better decision-making
