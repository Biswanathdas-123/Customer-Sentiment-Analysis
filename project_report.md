# Customer Sentiment Analysis Project Report

## Purpose

This project analyzes customer reviews to identify customer sentiment and understand overall customer satisfaction.

## Business Problem

Businesses receive a large number of customer reviews every day. Understanding customer opinions helps improve products, services, and customer experience.

## Methodology

1. Collect customer reviews
2. Prepare and organize the dataset
3. Analyze customer feedback
4. Classify reviews as Positive, Negative, or Neutral
5. Summarize customer insights

## Expected Results

- Positive Reviews
- Negative Reviews
- Neutral Reviews
- Customer Satisfaction Insights

## Dataset

Customer Reviews Dataset

## Conclusion

Customer Sentiment Analysis helps businesses understand customer opinions and make better business decisions using customer feedback.
